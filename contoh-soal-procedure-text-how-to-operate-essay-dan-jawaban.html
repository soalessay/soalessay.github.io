2. berikan contoh text procedure how to operate computer


Contoh teks procedure "how to operate computer":

Operating a computer:

First, make sure that the computer CPU is connected to the power source which here in Indonesia is 220VAC. After the computer CPU is connected to the power source, switch the power button on. You can find the power button on the front side of the CPU and wait until the computer finishes the booting process. Next, the computer will show the start menu for Windows operating system and will ask you to input the password for your Windows account.

Pembahasan

Procedure text di atas bisa kita terjemahkan sebagai berikut.

Pastikan terlebih dahulu bahwa CPU komputer terhubung ke sumber listrik yang di Indonesia adalah 220VAC. Setelah CPU komputer tersambung ke sumber listrik, tekan tombol on. Anda bisa menemukan tombol on di sisi depan CPU dan tunggu hingga komputer menyelesaikan proses booting. Selanjutnya, komputer akan menampilkan menu start untuk sistem operasi Windows dan akan meminta Anda memasukkan kata sandi untuk akun Windows Anda.

5. Procedure text how to make jasuke?


Procedure text on How to make a Jasuke (Jagung Susu Keju) or Corn, Milk and Cheese:

Ingredient:

Corn kernelsA tabelspoon butter and margarineSweetened condensed milkCheddar Cheese

Step by step instructions:

First of all, you have to steam the corn until it cooked completely. Meanwhile, you grate the cheddar cheese. When the corn is cooked, you then mix it with the butter or if you like, with a margarine and little salt. After that pour the corn into the prepared cup and add the milk, while you can sprinkle it with the cheese. Finally the delicious jasuke is ready.

Pembahasan

Di dalam soal di atas kita diminta untuk membuat sebuah procedure text (teks prosedur) tentang bagaimana cara membuat makanan ringan yang sangat lezat, yaitu jasuke atau singkatan dari jagung susu keju. Di dalam sebuah teks prosedur bahasa atau tenses yang digunakan adalah simple present tense, jadi penting kamu ingat untuk selalu menggunakan simple present tense setiap kali membuat sebuah teks prosedur. Di dalam sebuah procedure text ada 3 bagian utama yaitu bagian ingredients (bahan-bahan), bagian tools (alat-alat) bila ada, dan bagian steps (langkah-langkah), sedangkan di dalam teks dalam jawaban di atas hanya ada dua bagian yaitu ingredients dan steps.

Making a Car with a Plastic Bottle

1

Clean the plastic bottle. Take the label off the bottle. Let the bottle soak in hot water and dish soap for ten minutes. This will make it easier to remove any residue left on the bottle as well as cleaning off any bacteria.

2

Drill the two holes into each side of the bottle. These holes will be where the axles will go. The holes should be parallel with each other.[1]

 

3

Find your axles. You can use a variety of objects for your axles: straws, sticks, toothpicks, or wire (like that from a wire hanger), etc. If the object is long, like a pencil, then you will only need two pieces. If it is short, like a toothpick, then you will need four pieces.

 

4

Gather four bottle caps. You will use these caps as your wheels.

5

Paint the car and wheels. You can paint the outside of the bottle and the wheels. It’s easier to paint bottle and bottle caps before you fully assemble the car.

6

Place the axles into the plastic bottle. Depending on the material you chose, this might be two or four axles. For a longer object, run the axle all the way through the set of holes on the other side. For a shorter object, like a toothpick, place one in each hole.

7

Drill a hole into the lid. Knot a string and then thread it through the lid. Make sure the knot is on the inside of the lid. Screw the lid onto the plastic bottle.

8

Cut the top of the bottle to make a windshield. Using a sharp object like a box cutter, cut a rectangle or square into the top of the bottle. Only cut three sides of the shape so that you can fold it up. Cut it so that when you flip it up the windshield faces the lid of the bottle.

9

Drill a hole through each of the four plastic lids. Using a drill or sharp object, create a hole in the center of the lids.

10

Place the bottle caps onto the axles of the car. Run the axles through the holes you drilled in the bottle caps. See how the car sits. If the lids are too big or too small, then the car may not move. Have the top of the lids face the car so that it has more stability.

11

Use the string to pull the car. If you don’t want to use the string, then you can push the car to make it move.

How to make a glass of coffee:

Ingredients

1 teaspoon of coffee 1½ spoons of cream½ teaspoon of sugar A cup of water (300 ml)

Steps:

First, heat 300 ml of water until it boils. Mix the coffee grounds and cream into the cup. After the water boils, pour it into the cup containing coffee and cream. Stir gently until the coffee dissolves in the water and the cream mixes with the coffee. The color of the coffee will be brown. Sekilas mengenai penjelasan singkat dari procedure text, dalam bahasa inggris, procedure text is the text to explain how to do something. Dengan kata lain, procedure text merupakan sebuah text yang digunakan untuk menunjukan cara melakukan atau membuat sesuatu dalam bahasa inggris.

Tujuan dari procedure text yaitu untuk menggambarkan suatu perintah atau instruksi dalam bahasa inggris. Struktur procedure text yaitu Material (bahan), equipment (peralatan) dan step (langkah).

Berikut contoh procedure text yang berhubungan dengan how to operate dalam bahasa inggris.

PROCEDURE TEXT

How to Use Computer.

Set up your computer. If you set up a new computer, there are a few steps that you will need before you can use it

Atur komputer Anda. Jika Anda mengatur komputer baru, ada beberapa langkah yang akan Anda butuhkan sebelum Anda dapat menggunakannya

Create a user account. If you use a computer for the first time, you will be asked to create a user account when you turn it on.

Buatlah user account. Jika Anda menggunakan komputer untuk pertama kalinya, Anda akan diminta untuk membuat user account ketika Anda menyalakannya.

Get to know the desktop. Desktop is the main work area on your computer, and will often be areas that you visit on your computer

Mengenal desktop. Dekstop merupakan area kerja utama pada komputer Anda, dan akan sering menjadi area yang dikunjungi pada komputer Anda

Learn the basic use of the mouse and keyboard. The mouse and keyboard is the main tools to interact with your computer.

Pelajari penggunaan dasar mouse dan keyboard. Mouse dan keyboard merupakan peralatan utama untuk berinteraksi dengan komputer Anda.

Launch a few applications will be installed. Even if you assemble your own computer, there will be some applications that will be installed and the equipment can be used without having to install additional applications on its own

Meluncurkan beberapa aplikasi yang akan dipasang. Bahkan jika Anda merakit komputer Anda sendiri, akan ada beberapa aplikasi yang akan dipasang dan peralatan yang dapat digunakan tanpa harus memasang tambahan aplikasi sendiri

How to Cook Fried Rice

>Ingredients:

-2 cups of cooked rice

-5 chilies or more (depends on your spicy level)

-1 clove of garlic

-2 onions

-2 tablespoons of butter

-1 egg, slightly beaten

-Soy sauce

-Salt

>Steps of cooking fried rice:

1) First, puree the garlic, onion, and chilies with the help of pestle, or you can use blender as an alternative way.

2) Next, heat the butter on frying pan and wait for a while until it’s melted.

3) Then, pour the puree seasoning and stir-fry.

4) After that, add the slightly beaten egg and mix it well.

5) Next, add salt and soy sauce.

Pour the cooked rice and mix them well.

6) Check the taste whether it’s tasty enough or not, add salt if it’s less salty.

7) Finally, the simple fried rice is ready to serve.

37. procedure text how to make puding


How to Make Chocolate pudding
Material :
- Agar-Agar White 1 Pack
- Milk 400 Ml Liquid
- 50 gr Dark Cooking Chocolate, Chopped
- 2 Tablespoons Granulated Sugar
- 1 Egg Yolk, Beaten
- Egg Whites 3 Eggs 

Tools:Pan, Bowl, Stove, Spoon, Mold, Refrigerator, Water
Steps:

First, Agar-agar cook with milk, chocolate and sugar over medium heat until hot.
Second, take a little dough, then mix with the egg yolks, pour back into the hot mixture. Cook until boiling. Lift.
Next, Beaten egg whites until fluffy and up. Then pour the hot gelatin mixture gradually while stirring constantly until well blended. 
Then, Save or pour into a mold. Allow it to freeze, so quickly refrigerate until cold. 
Finally, prepared pudding served with a complement.
