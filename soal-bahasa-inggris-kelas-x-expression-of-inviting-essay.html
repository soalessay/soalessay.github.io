9. Buatlah dialog berbahasa inggris 5 orang yang mengandung expression of introduction , expression of planning, expression of suggestion, expression of opinion, expression of offering help/servis​


Jawaban:

Beni : Good morning to the audience, today we gather to discuss the problem of waste. Before that, let's thank God Almighty because today we can gather healthy and safe. So who wants to propose an opinion for the problem this time?

Edo : Hm... It seems like a lot of people throw garbage because they don't care about the environment so we should put up posters.

Dodo : Right, I also suggest that we provide trash bins everywhere.

Qiqi: Hmm... yes, I also suggest that we give fines to people who throw away trash so they don't throw garbage anymore.

Keke : I agree with all of your opinions, let's start implementing it now.

Beni : Wait! Then any other opinion? if not let's start.

Keke : Edo can I help you make a poster? I think it looks like the poster that will be made should be more interesting.

Edo : Yes. You're right.

Beni : Well let's do it!

Penjelasan:

Ungkapan pengantar ( expression of introduction ) adalah salah satu bagian awal (pendahuluan) dari sebuah karya ilmiah. Umumnya, di dalam kata pengantar ini penulis mengucapkan rasa syukur . ungkapan perencanaan ( expression of planning ) Contohnya " Mari "ungkapan saran ( expression of suggestion ) berisi saran contohnya "Saya sarankan"ungkapan pendapat ( expression of opinion ) Berisi pendapat contohnya " Saya rasa... "ungkapan menawarkan bantuan/layanan ( expression of offering help/servis ) Contohnya " Bisakah saya membantumu?"
