Penjelasan:

1. am going to

adalah future tense yang berarti setelah kata itu harus Verb 1

RUMUS = S + will/tobe going to + V1 + o/c

2. don't = modal

setelah kata don't haruslah verb 1

RUMUS = S + do/does + V1 + o/c

3. assignment = tugas

4. have you

have = perfect tense

yang dimana harus menggunakan verb 3

RUMUS = S + has/have + V3 + o/c

5. will be waiting

kenapa be? karena itu adalah kata bantu

setelah will kan pakai V1 kenapa itu bisa Verb ing?

Itu adalah contoh kalimat future continuous tense yang mana harus ditambah kata bantu be

RUMUS = S + will + be + verb ing + o/c

37. kak tolong bantuin yaa bing kls 9​


Jawaban:

Present Continuous Tense

Positive sentence

+ He is helping my mother now

+ She is writing a story book this month

+ The children are studying math at this moment

Negative sentence

- He is not helping my mother now

- She is not writing a story book this month

- The children are not studying mat at this moment

Interrogative sentence

Is he helping my mother now?

Is she writing a story book this month?

Are The children studying math at this moment?

Rumus Present Continuous Tense

Positive

Subject + to be + Verb-ing + Object/Adverb

Negative

Subject + to be + not + Verb-ing + Object/Adverb

Interrogative

To be + Subject + Verb-ing + Object/Adverb + ?

To Be = am/is/are
