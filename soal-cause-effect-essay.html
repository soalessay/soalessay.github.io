9. write a brief cause-effect essay about eating too much sweet foodbantuujan ngasalmakasii ​


Jawaban:

1. Causes tooth decay

Sugar which is often found in candy and some other sweet foods can cause tooth decay in children. Bacteria in the mouth will grow quickly if the main food needs, namely sugar, are met properly.

These bacteria then damage the teeth and the effects will last into adulthood. This problem may seem trivial, but poor oral and dental health has been linked to various risks of serious diseases later in life, such as heart disease.

2. Fulfillment of poor nutrition

The main problem that is often experienced by children who love sweet foods is that they tend to replace all other forms of food with sweet-tasting foods. Although sugary foods provide energy that allows children to be more active, sugar actually has very low nutritional value.

Even though they donate large amounts of calories, children's nutritional needs will not be met by consuming sweet foods. Fulfillment of poor nutrition has a major effect on growth and development.

3. Increases the risk of diabetes

When children are exposed to more sugar, insulin levels in the body will increase. This can harm the pancreas and increase the risk of diabetes at an early age, namely teenagers. In addition, too much sugar can also increase insulin resistance and the body's ability to lower blood sugar levels is impaired.

4. Causes of obesity

Weight gain is a big problem because it has several bad effects on health, and sugar is one of the big contributors to weight gain. Unused sugar in the body can cause fat deposits.

Obesity due to this fat accumulation since childhood will be difficult to overcome when it is an adult by means of a regular diet.

5. Increase blood pressure

Too much sugar can also increase the risk of high blood pressure. According to a study involving stroke patients with normal and high blood pressure, the chances of survival are very low in patients with high blood sugar levels.

6. Increases the risk of heart disease

Sweet foods can affect a person's cholesterol levels. Although research does not directly prove that sugar can affect cholesterol levels, researchers do show that children who consume large amounts of sugar in their diet are found to have higher levels of cholesterol, a cause of heart disease.

7. Promotes the growth of cancer cells

Several studies have shown that cancer cells will grow rapidly due to exposure to sugary foods. Sugary foods can increase insulin levels in the body which in turn, promotes the growth of cancer cells.

21. bikin tugas ni .. tlong dong bntuannya bikin essay dari cause and effect .. terimakasih


Discoveries and invention of devices  are always welcome till we, humans, find a way to abuse its benefits and be adversely affected by it. This was the case when Wilhelm Roentgen discovered x-ray and within five years, the British Army was using a mobile x-ray unit to locate bullets and shrapnel in wounded soldiers in the Sudan. TV was also invented with positive thoughts in mind – there would be no national borders, education and communication would be worldwide, etc. However, we are now trying to overcome its physiological and psychological adverse effects on human beings. One of the physiological effects of watching TV in excessive amounts is eye-strain. It is true that there are specifications for watching TV; TV should be 5 m. away from the eye, the room should be adequately lit, TV should be placed at the same height with our eyes, etc. However, these do not prevent our eyes from getting tired if we keep watching TV for a long time. Another effect is obesity, which is widely observed in people who like watching TV and eating snacks  everyday (there is even a term “TV snacks” to refer to fast food that is suitable for eating in front of the TV). TV is such a powerful machine that people cannot get away from it – it is addictive. Apart from the physiological effects, TV also causes psychological effects. One  is a result of being exposed to violence. After seeing so many violent scenes on TV, people start considering violent actions normal and they lose their sensitivity to their environment. Partly connected to this effect, the interpersonal communication among people decreases. Being insensitive to the suffering of other people causes people to become alienated. Also, after coming home from work people seek to relax in front of the TV, and generally people prefer watching TV to talking to each other. This issue is very important since lack of interpersonal relationships mostly end with divorces. Shortly, inventions are meant to be beneficial for human beings, if we know how to benefit from them. TV is one of such inventions that need to be used for the right purpose only – being educated and entertained for a reasonable (according to age) period of time. We may, then, be safe from or at least reduce the adverse physiological and psychological effects of watching too much TV.

23. contoh soal pilihan ganda bahasa inggris cause and effect


Cause and Effect

Anna: Hey Lulu, I recognize that your face is looking brighter and your look slimmer! What’s the secret?

Lulu: Well, thanks for noticing Ann. For the past months I have been working out regularly at least three times a week. Thanks to yoga!

Anna: Wow, sounds very interesting.

Lulu: Yes, but not only it. I also have been taking care of my food. Due to my lactose intolerant, I try to avoid dairy as much as possible. And it works for me!

Anna: I’m very inspired Ann! As a result of my laziness and eating too much junk food, I gained 5 kilograms in a month! I need to get back in shape and do more workout.

Lulu: Yes, I support you!

Simak dialog di atas untuk menjawab soal-soal pilihan ganda berikut.

A. Thanks to

B. Because of

C. Due to

D. As a result of

2. “As a result of my laziness and eating too much junk food, I gained 5 kilograms in a month!”

Which one is the effect?

A. Becoming lazy

B. Eating too much junk food

C. Gained 5 kilograms in a month

D. Lose 5 kilograms in a month

3. What is the cause that Lulu’s face is looking brighter and she looks slimmer?

A. Yoga three times a week

B. Eating junk food

C. Drinking more dairy

D. Gain 5 kilograms

Pembahasan

Untuk mengungkapkan cause and effect dalam bahasa Inggris, kita menggunakan signal words berikut:

due tobecause ofowing tothanks toas a consequence ofas a result ofbecausesinceasfor

Nah, untuk menunjukan cause and effect dalam kalimat ada dua jenis yakni yang diikuti kata benda (followed by noun) dan yang diikuti kata kerja (followed by verb).

Followed by noun

Signal word/phrase + Cause + Effect

Effect + Signal word/phrase + Cause

Due to Jen’s laziness, she didn’t pass the Math test.

Signal phrase: Due toCause: Jen’s lazinessEffect: She didn’t pass the test

He was sick as a consequence of his ignorance.

Signal phrase: As a consequenceCause: His ignoranceEffect: He was sick

Followed by verb

Signal word/phrase + Cause + Effect

Effect + Signal word/phrase + Cause

Because Jen was lazy, she didn’t pass the Math test.

Signal phrase: BecauseCause: Jen was lazyEffect: She didn’t pass the Math test

As he ignored his health, he will be sick,

Signal word: AsCause: He ignoredEffect: He will be sick

1. Question: What causes acid rain and what is its impact on the environment?

Answer: Acid rain is caused by the emission of pollutant gases such as sulfur dioxide and nitrogen oxides into the atmosphere that react with rainwater. As a result, acid rain can damage soil, water, and plants, and can negatively impact human and animal health.

2. Question: What causes global warming and what is its impact on the world's climate?

Answer: Global warming is caused by an increase in greenhouse gas emissions such as carbon dioxide. The result is an increase in the Earth's average temperature, leading to climate changes like increased intensity of extreme weather and rising sea levels.

3. Question: What causes deforestation and what is its impact on biodiversity?

Answer: Deforestation is often caused by the clearing of forests for agriculture, plantations, or mining. The result is that deforestation can reduce the natural habitats of forest species, threatening biodiversity.

4. Question: What causes forest fires and what is their impact on air quality?

Answer: Forest fires are often caused by human activities, such as logging and land clearing by burning. The result is that forest fires can produce smoke and particles that damage air quality and impact human health.

5. Question: What causes water pollution and what is its impact on marine life?

Answer: Water pollution is often caused by the disposal of industrial or household waste into water. The result is that water pollution can damage water ecosystems, reduce water quality, and negatively impact marine life.

6. Question: What causes obesity and what is its impact on health?

Answer: Obesity is usually caused by unhealthy eating habits and lack of physical activity. The result is that obesity can increase the risk of various diseases, such as diabetes, heart disease, and certain types of cancer.

7. Question: What causes the increase in the use of renewable energy and what is its impact on the environment?

Answer: The increase in the use of renewable energy is often due to concerns about climate change and the depletion of fossil fuel sources. The result is that the use of renewable energy can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions and has a lower environmental impact compared to fossil energy.

8. Question: What causes the decline in bee populations and what is its impact on agriculture?

Answer: The decline in bee populations is often caused by the use of pesticides, climate change, and loss of habitat. The result is that a decline in bee populations can reduce pollination, which is important for agriculture and food production.

9. Question: What causes the increase in the use of digital technology and what is its impact on society?

Answer: The increase in the use of digital technology is often due to technological advancements and the need to increase efficiency and productivity. The result is that the use of digital technology can affect the way we work, learn, and interact with others.

10. Question: What causes stress and what is its impact on mental health?

Answer: Stress can be caused by various factors, such as work pressure, financial problems, or personal issues. The result is that stress can affect mental and physical health, including an increased risk of depression, anxiety, and heart disease.

PEMBAHASAN:

Pada dasarnya antara because of dan owing to memiliki makna yang sama dan sama- sama preposition yang mana bisa dimaknai sebagai kata penghubung pula, yang menjelaskan alasan atau mengungkapkan ‘karena apa’ sesuatu terjadi  dan diikuti oleh kata benda, bukan klausa.

Cause and Effect

Cause adalah:

The reason or motive, for an action Why something happen Happen first

Effect adalah:

Result or conclusion of an action What happened Happens due to a cause

Jadi cause and effect biasa juga disebut dengan kalimat sebab-akibat atau kalimat kausalitas adalah kalimat yang dibangun dengan melibatkan tindakan yang membuat sesuatu terjadi dan hasil dari tindakan tersebut. Paling gampang ditandai dengan kata hubung yang menandakan sebab akibat.

Signal words of cause

Ketika kita bicara tentang cause maka kata- kata yang dapat kita gunakan adalah sebagai berikut:

Because  the reason for  On account of  Bring about  Give rise to f Created by  Contributed to  Led to  Due to  For this reason  Unless

Signal words of Effect

Ketika kita bicara tentang effect maka kata- kata yang dapat kita gunakan adalah sebagai berikut:

As a result  Then  Hence  For this reason Outcome  So  Consequently  Finally  Therefore  In order to

The sentence structure of cause and effect

Due to/Because of/owing to/Thanks to + noun Because/Since/As/For/As a result of + subject + verb

Contoh:

Owing to her hard work and intelligence, we won the trophy.  (Berkat kerja keras dan kecerdasannya, kami memenangkan trofi.)Thanks to Ammar and Ameera’s effective planning, the event went well. ( Berkat perencanaan efektif Ammar dan Ameera, acara ini berjalan dengan baik)Because of his hard work, he managed to get the best student award . (Karena kerja kerasnya, ia berhasil mendapatkan penghargaan siswa terbaik)I have  a stomach ache because I ate too much food . (Perut saya sakit karena makan terlalu banyak)There was a lot of homework and test, as a result most of the students were unhappy and couldn’t go anywhere during the weekend.  (Ada banyak pekerjaan rumah dan ujian, akibatnya sebagian besar siswa tidak bahagia dan tidak bisa pergi ke mana pun selama akhir pekan.)Since Iqbal has been promoted as a manager so he always does his best in everything (Karena Iqbal telah dipromosikan sebagai manajer, maka ia selalu melakukan yang terbaik dalam segala hal)

Note : Hal yang perlu diperhatikan dalam membentuk kalimat cause and effect  adalah jika kata hubung terletak di awal kalimat, maka kita perlu menggunakan tanda baca koma. Namun, jika kata hubungnya berada di tengah, kita tidak perlu menggunakan koma.
