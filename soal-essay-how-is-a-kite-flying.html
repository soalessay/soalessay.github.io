10. how to make a kite??


(How to make diamond kite):
Materials: scissors, a ruler, three garbage bags, a pencil, tape and two wooden dowels. (The dowel for the spine should be 10 millimeters thick and 95 centimeters long, the dowel for the spar should be 6 millimeters thick and 90 cm long). You should also have a roll of string.
How to make:
1. Using the garbage bags, make a 1x1 meter plastic sheet. If the garbage bags are too small, then cut them open and tape a few together using strong tape. Draw on the lines that represent the spar and spine as shown in the picture according to the dimensions. If you don't want to draw then tape on some string and use them as guide lines. (remember to take off after cutting)
2. Once youve drawn on the two lines that represent the spar and spine. Join the ends of the lines to form a diamond shape. Again if you don't want to draw, just tape on some string and use that as guide lines. (remember to take them off once finished cutting)
3.Cut out your sail
