6. Apa pengertian food and drink label


Jawaban:

Label makanan dan minuman (food and drink label) adalah informasi yang tercetak atau ditampilkan pada kemasan produk makanan atau minuman. Label ini berisi informasi tentang komposisi produk, nilai gizi, bahan-bahan, tanggal kadaluwarsa, petunjuk penyimpanan, informasi alergen, dan kadang-kadang juga klaim kesehatan atau keunggulan produk. Tujuan dari label makanan dan minuman adalah untuk memberikan konsumen informasi yang diperlukan untuk membuat pilihan yang cerdas dan sadar terkait produk yang akan mereka konsumsi.

Jawaban:

Food and drink label (label makanan dan minuman) adalah informasi yang dicetak atau ditulis pada kemasan produk makanan atau minuman yang memberikan detail tentang isi, bahan, nutrisi, tanggal kedaluwarsa, cara penyimpanan, dan informasi lain yang relevan bagi konsumen.

Penjelasan:

"Food and Drink Label". Printed or written information on a food or drink product packaging that provides details about its contents, ingredients, nutrition, expiry date, storage instructions, and other relevant information for consumers.

25. Kinds of Food and Drink Food and drink are important for people in daily life. Here are the common kinds of food and drink, as follows. Kinds of food and drink Fried chicken Meatball Roaster Rice Fried rice Porridge Ketchup Material Summary Salt Sugar Honey Juice Hot tea Chocolate drink Iced tea Kinds of food and drink Pizza Spaghetti Kebab Fresh water Cheese Candy Egg Omelette Boiled egg Boiled noodle Fried noodle Milk Coffee Toast​


Food and drink are indeed important for people in daily life, as they provide energy, nutrients, hydration, and enjoyment.

Some of the food and drink you mentioned are typical of certain regions or cultures, such as pizza and spaghetti from Italy, kebab from the Middle East, fried chicken and ketchup from the United States, and fried rice and fried noodle from Asia. Some of them are also influenced by the availability of ingredients, such as fresh water, cheese, honey, and egg. Some of them are prepared in different ways, such as roaster, boiled, fried, or omelette.

Food and drink can also reflect one’s personality, mood, or occasion. For example, some people may prefer hot tea or coffee in the morning to wake up, while others may enjoy iced tea or chocolate drink in the afternoon to relax. Some people may like to eat candy or cheese as a snack, while others may prefer meatball or egg as a meal. Some people may choose pizza or kebab for a party, while others may opt for rice or porridge for a comfort food.

Food and drink are also related to health and wellness. Some of them are more nutritious than others, such as fresh water, juice, milk, honey, egg, and cheese. Some of them are more caloric than others, such as fried chicken, pizza, spaghetti, and chocolate drink. Some of them are more suitable for certain dietary needs or preferences, such as vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, or halal.
