Jawaban:

1. Procedure text is a text that give us instructions to do or make something.

2. The purpose of procedure text is to tell us how to make / operate something based on the sequence of steps.

3. – Goal : showing the purpose

– Materials : Telling the needed materials

– Steps : The ways to make / how to use something

4. Imperative ( Kalimat perintah ) example : stir it

Simple Present Tense ( s + v1)

Temporal conjunctions example : first, then

Action verbs ( Kata kerja ) example : cook, bake

Materials : Television and Remote

2. Second, press the power button on your television remote. If you don't have your remote you can search the turn on button on your television.

3. Last, wait until your television screen starts and choose the channel you want to watch on your television. Enjoy the show on your television.

21. bantu jawab soal ini1. What is procedure text?2. What is the generic structure of procedure text?3. What is the language features of procedure text?4. Mention kinds of procedure text.​


Jawaban:

1. Procedure text is a type of text that provides instructions or directions on how to perform a specific task or process. It is commonly used to explain a sequence of steps in order to achieve a desired outcome.

2. The generic structure of a procedure text typically consists of three main parts:

a. Goal/aim: This part introduces the purpose or objective of the procedure, explaining what the reader will be able to achieve by following the instructions.

b. Materials/equipment: This part lists the necessary tools, ingredients, or equipment needed to carry out the procedure.

c. Steps: This part presents a series of sequential instructions or steps that need to be followed in order to complete the procedure successfully.

3. The language features commonly found in procedure text include:

a. Imperative verbs: These are used to give direct commands or instructions, such as "mix," "cut," "stir," "boil," etc.

b. Simple present tense: The instructions are usually written in the present tense to indicate that they are general and applicable at any time.

c. Time connectors: Words like "first," "next," "then," "after that," and "finally" are used to indicate the order of the steps.

d. Adverbs and adverbial phrases: These are used to provide additional information about how the actions should be performed, such as "carefully," "gently," "slowly," etc.

e. Technical terms: Procedure texts often include specific vocabulary related to the task or process being described.

4. There are various kinds of procedure texts, including:

a. Recipes: These provide instructions on how to prepare and cook specific dishes.

b. Manuals: These provide instructions on how to operate or assemble a particular device or machine.

c. Instructions for using software or applications: These guide users on how to navigate and utilize specific software or applications.

d. Scientific experiments: These outline the steps to conduct scientific experiments and record observations.

e. DIY (Do-It-Yourself) projects: These provide instructions on how to create or build something, such as crafts or home improvement projects.

f. Guidelines for performing certain tasks: These offer instructions on how to perform tasks like CPR, first aid, or driving a car.

Procedure text merupakan jenis teks yang bertujuan memberi petunjuk cara mengoperasikan menciptakan, atau membuat sesuatu melalui serangkaian tindakan atau langkah.

Ciri kebahasaan procedure text:

Menggunakan kata kerja tindakan (action verb).Menggunakan pola kalimat perintah (imperrative).Menggunakan kata hubung (conjunction) untuk mengurutkan kegiatan.Mengunakan kata keterangan (adverb) untuk menyatakan rincian waktu.

Struktur procedure text:

Goal/Aim/Purpose: tujuan kegiatan atau judul.Materials/Ingredients: alat atau bahan yang diperlukan.Steps/Methods: langkah-langkah kegiatan secara utuh.

Ingredients

1 teaspoon of coffee 1½ spoons of cream½ teaspoon of sugar A cup of water (300 ml)

Steps:

Jawaban:

Procedure text is a text that explains or helps us how to make or use something. Its social function is to describe how something is completely done through a sequence of steps. Communicative purpose of this text is to describe how something is made through a sequence of actions or steps. There are three generic structures in procedure text. The first is goals or purposes. The second is materials or tools. The third and the last are steps or methods. To know the text that we read is procedure or not is so simple. You can read the title if the title is started with ‘how to make…’ or ‘how to use…’ it can be certainly defined that the text is procedure text.

34. What is "Procedure Text"? Why someone wrote the "Procedure Text" ? (Purpose of the text)


Procedure text (teks prosedur) merupakan suatu jenis teks yang isinya adalah teknik atau langkah-langkah melakukan, membuat, menghilangkan, menghapus, membangun, menciptakan sesuatu dan sebagainya.

Tujuan procedure text adalah untuk memberi solusi kepada pembaca mengenai masalah yang dihadapi, dalam hal ini menyelesakan masalah dengan cara-cara tertentu.

PEMBAHASAN

  Salah satu dari 13 jenis teks dalam Bahasa Inggris adalah teks prosedur (procedure text). Teks prosedur memberikan informasi yang dibutuhkan oleh pembaca mengenai solusi terhadap masalah yang sedang dihadapi.

  Judul teks prosedur dalam Bahasa Inggris umumnya menggunakan kata kunci "How to ... ". Contohnya:

How to install an app. (Bagaimana cara memasang sebuah aplikasi),How to fix a broken bicycle. (Bagaimana cara memperbaiki sepeda yang rusak), How to make an orange juice. (Bagaimana cara membuat jus jeruk), dll.

  Struktur teks prosedur (The Generic Structure of Procedure Text) adalah sebagai berikut.

Goal: berisi tentang tujuan dibuatnya teks prosedur tersebut.Materials and/or Ingredients: berisi tentang bahan-bahan dan/atau alat-alat yang dibutuhkan.Steps: berisi tentang langkah-langkah atau proses menyelesaikan suatu problem.

  Unsur kebahasaan dari teks prosedur (The Language Features of Procedure Text) adalah sebagai berikut.

Menggunakan kata keterangan urutan (Adverbs of Sequence): contohnya adalah first, second, third, next, before, after, finally.Menggunakan kata keterangan yang menunjukkan detail waktu: contohnya adalah three minutes, one hour, overnight, dll.Menggunakan kalimat perintah/petunjuk: contohnya adalah put some sugar into it, don't water the plants unless it is really necessary, wait until the water boils, dll.Menggunakan kata kerja Simple Present Tense: rumus umum kata kerja Simple Present Tense adalah Subject + Verb 1.

  Tujuan dibuatnya teks prosedur adalah untuk memberikan informasi mengenai langkah cara membuat sesuatu, memberi solusi terhadap masalah yang sedang dihadapi oleh pembaca, mengajari pembaca cara melakukan hal tertentu, dan lain sebagainya.
