15. contoh showing care, compliment, dan intention


showing care = menunjukkan kepedulian atau rasa simpati

Contoh:
- I'm sorry to hear that news.
- I know how you feel.
- Please accept my condolences.

compliment = memberikan pujian

Contoh:
- You are awesome.
- Nothing can beat you!
- That dress looks nice on you.

Intention = menyampaikan maksud atau tujuan (saya kurang paham maksudnya contoh intention seperti apa)

Contoh:
- I want to market this product globally.
- My intention is to get the best score.
- My goal is to make my parents happy.

31. Differences between showing care and compliment?


Showing care is an expression or feeling of pity and sorrow when we know and see people are unlucky, have trouble, or are in bad condition

Compliment is an expression to appreciate or praise other people
