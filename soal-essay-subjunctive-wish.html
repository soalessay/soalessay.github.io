21. explain about subjunctive!


Subjunctive digunakan untuk mengekspresikan sesuatu yang kita inginkan/bayangkan. Contohnya, subjunctive digunakan untuk:
- membicarakan sesuatu yang kita inginkan supaya terjadi.
- sesuatu yang kita antisipasi
- membayangkan sesuatu.

Subjunctive ini jarang digunakan dalam percakapan dan lebih banyak ditemukan pada teks. Ciri-ciri yang menonjol pada subjunctive adalah penggunaan infinitive yang tidak pernah berubah apapun subjeknya. 

Penggunaan:
1. Fixed subjunctives
contoh: Long live the King! 
God save the Queen. 

2. That + subjunctive 
Digunakan untuk mengindikasikan bahwa hal tsb harus dilakukan (directive)
Contoh: The boss demands that you be present at the meeting.
It is important that every student study. (walaupun every student merupakan subjek singular, yang digunakan adalah study bukan studies.)

subjunctive is another way of talking about possible, desirable, or hypothetical situations is by using what many grammars call a present subjunctive verb as in this example: It is necessary that Peter see his teacher. What makes the bolded verb special is that it does not have the third person singular –s ending that we find in declarative clauses that contain a present tense verb (e.g. Every day Peter sees his teacher).

Subjunctive for unreal present situation

1.The teacher wishes (that) all student came on time.  

2. The students wish (that) they could do the test.  

3. Shinta wishes (that) she passed the examination.  

4. Tomy wishes (that) he could speak English.  

Subjunctive for unreal past situation

1.The teacher wishes (that) all student had come on time.  

2. The students wish (that) they could have done the test.  

3. Shinta wishes (that) she had passed the examination.  

4. Tomy wishes (that) he could have spoken English.  

---------------

EXPLANATION

Subjunctive adalah suatu grammatical mood yang digunakan untuk menyatakan harapan akan suatu kejadian yang tidak sesuai dengan kenyataan/pengandaian (unreal/hypothetical situation).

Past subjunctive

Jika unreal situationnya terjadi di masa sekarang (present) atau masa depan (future), konstruksi kalimatnya adalah:

wish/if only + S + preterite (v₂)

Examples:  

I wish he were here now.

If only he were here now.

I wish I had ten million dollars now.

If only I had ten million dollars now.

*Pada past subjunctive, kata kerja be selalu menggunakan bentuk were, bukan was.

Past Perfect Subjunctive

Jika unreal situationnya terjadi di masa lalu (past), konstruksi kalimatnya adalah:

wish/if only + S + had + past participle (v₃)

atau jika menggunakan modal  could/would/should

wish/if only + S + could/would/should + have + past participle

Examples:

I wish he had been there last night.

If only he had been there last night.

I wish he could have seen this.

If only he could have seen this.

Note: sering ada kekeliruan penggunaan istilah present dan past subjunctive.

Present subjunctive mengacu pada kalimat yang menyatakan keinginan, tuntutan, atau saran fromal.

Present subjunctive tidak menggunakan kata-kata seperti wish, if only, atau would rather karena tidak menyatakan harapan untuk sesuatu yang tidak mungkin terjadi.

Contoh present subjunctive:

I ask that he stop calling me.

I demand that she clean her room.

It is necessary that they finish the project on time.

It is vital that he be caught at once.

Subjunctive : I would rather the man hadn't visited me yesterday

Subjunctive for unreal present situation

1.The teacher wishes (that) all student came on time.  

2. The students wish (that) they could do the test.  

3. Shinta wishes (that) she passed the examination.  

4. Tomy wishes (that) he could speak English.  

Subjunctive for unreal past situation

1.The teacher wishes (that) all student had come on time.  

2. The students wish (that) they could have done the test.  

3. Shinta wishes (that) she had passed the examination.  

4. Tomy wishes (that) he could have spoken English.  

---------------

EXPLANATION

Subjunctive adalah suatu grammatical mood yang digunakan untuk menyatakan harapan akan suatu kejadian yang tidak sesuai dengan kenyataan/pengandaian (unreal/hypothetical situation).

Past subjunctive

Jika unreal situationnya terjadi di masa sekarang (present) atau masa depan (future), konstruksi kalimatnya adalah:

wish/if only + S + preterite (v₂)

Examples:  

I wish he were here now.

If only he were here now.

I wish I had ten million dollars now.

If only I had ten million dollars now.

*Pada past subjunctive, kata kerja be selalu menggunakan bentuk were, bukan was.

Past Perfect Subjunctive

Jika unreal situationnya terjadi di masa lalu (past), konstruksi kalimatnya adalah:

wish/if only + S + had + past participle (v₃)

atau jika menggunakan modal  could/would/should

wish/if only + S + could/would/should + have + past participle

Examples:

I wish he had been there last night.

If only he had been there last night.

I wish he could have seen this.

If only he could have seen this.

Note: sering ada kekeliruan penggunaan istilah present dan past subjunctive.

Present subjunctive mengacu pada kalimat yang menyatakan keinginan, tuntutan, atau saran fromal.

Present subjunctive tidak menggunakan kata-kata seperti wish, if only, atau would rather karena tidak menyatakan harapan untuk sesuatu yang tidak mungkin terjadi.

Contoh present subjunctive:

I ask that he stop calling me.

I demand that she clean her room.

It is necessary that they finish the project on time.

It is vital that he be caught at once.
