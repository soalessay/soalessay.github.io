He was hospitalized last month because of dengue fever Ardi some of his friends, relatives and neighbors visited him in the hospital

PEMBAHASAN

1. Apa yang paling diceritakan teks pada kita?

=> The text mainly tells us about Tommy was high fever hospitalized last month because he was fever

Penjelasan:

Teks berisi mengenai sakit demam Tommy sampai di rumah sakit

2. Apa ide pokok pada paragrap pertama?

=> The main idea of paragraph one is about Tommy says sorry to Ardi because he hasn’t written to Ardi for a long time.

Penjelasan:Ide pokoknya adalah pokok sebuah cerita hingga memiliki penjelas di kalimat selanjutnya.Nah pokok paragrap pertama adalah Tommy minta maaf ke Ardi,karena di kalimat selanjutnya menjelaskan penyebabnya.

3. Apa yg membuat Tommy di rumah sakit?

=> He was hospitalized last month because of dengue fever

Penjelasan:

Penyebab dia di bawa ke RS adalah demam tinggi

4. Siapa yg menerima surat?

=> ardi

Penjelasan:

Tertera jelas ada 2 nama

Ardi => penerimaTommy => pengirim

5. Siapa yg mengunjungi Tommy di rumah sakit?

=> some of his friends, relatives and neighbors visited him in the hospital

Penjelasan:

Yg mengunjungi Tommy adalah teman-temannya, kerabat dan tetangga
