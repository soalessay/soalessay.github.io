1. apa itu possesive adjective, possesive pronoun, dan apa itu reflexive pronoun


-Possessive adjective adalah determiner untuk menyatakan kepemilikan terhadap noun.

- Possessive Pronoun adalah kata ganti yang berfungsi untuk menunjukkan kepemilikan. Di dalam suatu kalimat, kata ini dapat menempati posisi sebagai subject, subject complement, atau direct object.

- Reflexive Pronoun adalah kata ganti yang digunakan untuk menyatakan bahwa subject (berupa orang atau hewan) menerima aksi dari verb (reciprocal action) pada suatu kalimat.

27. Subjective pronoun Objevtive pronoun Possesive pronounPossesive pornoun Example and explanation


A subjective pronoun acts as the subject of a sentence—it performs the action of the verb. The subjective pronouns are he, I, it, she, they, we, and you.

He spends ages looking out the window.

After lunch, she and I went to the planetarium.

Objective Pronouns

An objective pronoun acts as the object of a sentence—it receives the action of the verb. The objective pronouns are her, him, it, me, them, us, and you.

Cousin Eldred gave me a trombone.

Take a picture of him, not us!

Possessive Pronouns

A possessive pronoun tells you who owns something. The possessive pronouns are hers, his, its, mine, ours, theirs, and yours.

The red basket is mine.

Yours is on the coffee table.

Demonstrative Pronouns

A demonstrative pronoun points out a noun. The demonstrative pronouns are that, these, this, and those.

That is a good idea.

These are hilarious cartoons.

A demonstrative pronoun may look like a demonstrative adjective, but it is used differently in a sentence: it acts as a pronoun, taking the place of a noun.

29. buatlah kalimat Simple Sentece yang menggunakan subject pronoun, object pronoun, possesive adjectives, possesive pronoun?​


Simple  Sentence :

Subject Pronoun : I am a boy and Jesslyn is a girl.

Object Pronoun : He gave us the candy.

Possesive Adjective : My homework is being borrowed by Rina

Possesive Pronoun : This book is MINE

1) SUBJECT PRONOUNCE : subject / kata ganti pelaku yang selalu terletak pada awal kalimat

RUMUS : S + to be + Noun (ATAU) S + to be + Adjective

=> She is a dancer.

==> "She" sebagai subject

2) OBJECT PRONOUNCE : objek (kata ganti benda) dalam sebuah kalimat, sehingga diletakkan di akhir kalimat.

=> Mom bought me a cellphone.

==> "me" sebagai object

3) POSSESIVE ADJECTIVE : berfungsi untuk mengindikasi kepemilikan (kata ganti benda) yang selalu diikuti benda.

RUMUS : S + to be + Possesive Adjective + N

=> This is my bag.

4) POSSESSIVE PRONOUNS : kata ganti yang digunakan untuk mewakili atau menggantikan kepemilikan dari orang, benda atau binatang, yang tidak diikuti benda.

=> This red bag is your bag, the blue one is mine.

==> "mine" berfungsi sebagai kata ganti untuk "my bag"

you you

he

it
