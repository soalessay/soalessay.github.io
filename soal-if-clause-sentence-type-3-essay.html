1. If I study hard, I will pass the exam tomorrow.

3. If I eat too much, my stomach will get upset.
4. If my mom don't pick me up, I will go home by a taxi

1. If I had study hard, I would've been passed the exam by now
2. I wouldn't have been gone shopping if I had known that I still have one project left
3. My stomach wouldn't have been upset if I hadn't been too greedy
4. I wouldn't have been gone home if I had known that my mom was already waiting for me
5. He wouldn't have bought her that dress if he had realized that he had been saving up his money to buy a ticket concert

20. Penjelasan if clause sama conditional sentence


digunakan untuk kalimat pengandaian. contohnya, if i had money, i would buy  new car

conditional sentence adalah kalimat pengandaian , disebut kalimat pengandaian karena makna yang terkandung didalamnya bertentangan dengan makna sesungguhnya.

3 kalimat pengandaian
1. Probable condition digunakan untuk peristiwa yang mungkin terjadi jika syaratnya terpenuhi
2. Improbable condition digunakan untuk peristiwa yang kemungkinan terjadinya lebih kecil karena tidak sesuai dengan keadaan sekarang
3. Impossible condition digunakan untuk peristiwa yang tidak mungkin terjadi karena waktunya sudah lewat

28. Buatlah 2 kalimat if clause (conditional sentence) type 1,2,3 Rumus type 1: if s+v1, s+will+v1 Rumus type 2: if s+v2, s+would+v1 Rumus type 3: if s+had+v3, s+would have+v3 Plss tolong ya kakk


Type 1.

If she misses the bus, she will be late to school.

(Kalau dia ketinggalan bis, dia akan terlambat ke sekolah.)

If you make your brother cries, you will get trouble with your mom.

(Kalau kamu buat adik kamu menangis, kamu akan mendapat masalah dengan ibu kamu.)

Type 2.

If I were you, I would ask her to stop crying. (I'm not her, so I can't tell her to stop crying)

(Jika aku jadi kamu, aku akan minta dia untuk berhenti menangis.)

If my friend understood what our teacher said, she would teach me. (My friend doesn't understand, so she cannot teach me.)

(Jika temanku tahu apa yang guru kita terangkan, dia akan mengajarkanku)

Type 3

If I had had enough money, I would've bought Taylor Swift's ticket concert.

(Jika aku punya uang yang cukup, aku bisa saja beli tiket konser Taylor Swift)

If they had not warned us, bad thing would have happened to us.

(Jika mereka tidak memberitahu/memperingati kita, hal yang buruk bisa saja menimpa kepada kita.)

38. buatlah dialog tentang if clause type 0,1,2,dan 3​


Jawaban:

Contoh dialog conditional sentences type 0 (zero conditional):

Anne: What do you usually do on your holiday?

Rose: If I stay at home, I spend time with my sister.

Anne: You don’t go anywhere?

Rose: I go to the cinema if I my sister asks me.

Anne: What kind of movie you watch at cinema?

Rose: I always watch thriller whenever I go to cinema.

Anne: It sounds exciting.

Rose: It’s not exciting if my sister suddenly wants to watch another genre.

Contoh dialog conditional sentence type 1 (first conditional):

John: I can’t wait to see James at the final competition tonight.

Mark: Why?

John: If he wins, he will treat us next week.

Mark: Really?

John: Yes. Will you come tonight?

Mark: I will come if I get my father’s permission.

John: I will pick you up if you get your father’s permission, Mark.

Mark: Ok. Thanks, John.

Contoh dialog conditional sentence type 2 (second conditional):

Josh: My phone dropped when I got out of the car. I’m so relieved it’s not broken.

Ron: But the scratches are everywhere. If I were you, I would buy a new phone.

Josh: I would buy the new one if had more money.

Ron: What if you start saving?

Josh: I can’t since I just lost my job.

Ron: Don’t worry, Josh.

Josh: If I had a job, I would start saving.

Contoh dialog conditional sentence type 3 (third conditional):

Mary: Have you found your cat?

Kate: Yes, I have. I wouldn’t have found her if Shawn had not helped me.

Mary: What a relief! I’m sorry I couldn’t help you at that time.

Kate: It’s okay. By the way, how was holiday in France?

Mary: It was so exciting. If I my mother had not called me, I would have stayed longer.

Kate: Glad to hear that. I wish I had had money to go France with you.
