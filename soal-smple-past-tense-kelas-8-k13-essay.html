1. apa yang dimaksud smple past tense???


Simple past tense itu kata kerja sederhana yg menunjukkan bahwa suatu kejadian terjadi di masa lampau.

Rumus :
positif (+) :
S + Verb-2 (past tense)
S + be(was/were)

negatif (-) :
S + did + not + bare infinitive
S + be(was/were) + not

interogatif (?) :
Did + S + bare infinitive
be(was/were) + S

Contoh :
Vina and I went to the Ragunan zoo three months ago.

6. buatlah soal past tense + past perfect (5),past tense + past continous(5)


A. PAST TENSE VS PAST CONTINUOUS
1. My mother called me last night, when I was studying
2. The children were watching television, when their parents came home
3. You were making dinner, when my friends went home
4. When she was cleaning the windows, her husband called me
5. I was sleeping, when my brother woke me up

Note :
Gunakan PAST CONTINUOUS pada kalimat atau kejadian yang terjadi lebih dulu. Dalam bahasa Indonesia bisa diartikan "..... SEDANG MELAKUKAN".
Baik PAST TENSE dan PAST CONTINUOUS digunakan untuk kalimat yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau

B. PAST TENSE VS PAST PERFECT
1. When she was 21 years old, she had married
2. He had a luxury car, before he was 30 years old
3. They had had lunch, before they went to the library
4. Before we watched television, we had had done our homework
5. After they had gone to Paris, they went to England

Note :
Gunakan PAST PERFECT pada kalimat atau kejadian yang sudah selesai dikerjakan, dan dilanjutkan kejadian selanjutnya dimana kalimatnya menggunakan SIMPLE PAST. Dalam bahasa Indonesia, PAST PERFECT bisa diartikan ".... SUDAH MELAKUKAN".

20. Carilah materi tentang simple past tense kelas 8 SMP​


Jawaban:

Pengertian dan penggunaan

Bentuk past tense digunakan untuk menunjukkan kegiatan di masa lampau. Simple past tense sendiri adalah bentuk sederhana untuk menunjukkan kejadian di masa lampau.

Rumusnya

1.)Simple past nominal

Bentuk ini digunakan untuk kalimat yang diikuti oleh kata sifat (adjective), kata benda (noun), dan keterangan tempat (adverb place)

To be dalam bentuk past ada dua: Was dan were. Was digunakan untuk I, He, She, dan It. Were digunakan untuk You, They, dan We.

Rumusnya:

Positive: Subject + to be + noun/adj/adv.

Negative: Subject + to be + not + noun/adj/adv.

Interrogative: To be + subject + noun/adj/adv + ?

2.) Simple past verbal

Di dalam kalimat bentuk ini terdapat kata kerja atau verb. Verb yang digunakan adalah past tens verb atau Verb 2.  

Rumusnya:

Positive: Subject + verb 2.

Negative: Subject + did not + present verb/verb 1,

Interrogative: Did + subject + present verb/verb 1 + ?

Verb 2 ada yang berbentuk regular (beraturan) dan irregular (tidak beraturan). Untuk regular biasanya ditambah "-ed" di akhir kata. Anda perlu menghafalkan bentuk irregular verb karena bentuk present, past dan past perfect nya berbeda.

PART B

3). I was having dinner at 19.00 p.m last night.

PART C

1). After I had heard the news, I hurried to see him.

2). Before we went very far, we had found that we had lost our way.

3). I was sorry that I had hurt him.

4). Did you post the letter after you had wrote it?

5). Try not to be absent from class again for the rest of the term. You had already missed too many classes. You missed two classes just last week.

6). He said that he had sent the invitation letter before.

PEMBAHASAN

Simple past adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan tentang kejadian/perbuatan yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau.

Rumus :

(+) S + Verb-2 + O

(-) S + Did + Not + Verb-1 + O

(?) Did + S + Verb-1 + O

Tanda waktu :

YesterdayTwo (weeks) agoLast (year)Three (hours) agoEtc

Contoh :

I bought some books yesterday.I did not buy some books yesterday.Did I buy some books yesterday?

Past continuous adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan tentang kejadian/perbuatan yang sedang berlangsung pada masa lampau di saat peristiwa lain terjadi/dilakukan.

Rumus :

(+) S + To be (was/were) + Verb-ing + O

(-) S + To be (was/were) + not + Verb-ing + O

(?) To be (was/were) + S + Verb-ing + O

Tanda waktu :

....when.......as........while...All day yesterdayEtc

Contoh :

She was listening to the radio when I called her.While I was cooking, my sister did her homework.

Past perfect adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan tentang kejadian/perbuatan yang sudah selesai dilakukan pada waktu lampau.

Rumus :

(+) S + Had + Verb-3 + O

(-) S + Had + not + Verb-3 + O

(?) Had + S + Verb-3 + O

Tanda waktu :

....before........after......as soon as.......until...

Contoh :

Before I arrived, The train had left.She told me her name after I had asked her twice.
