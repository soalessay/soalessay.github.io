Untuk menentukan bahwa kalimat ini dan itu merupakan tipe ke 2 atau tipe ke 3, harap diperhatikan konteks kalimatnya atau cermati fakta nya dan bentuk kata kerja masing-masing klausa. Pada umumnya meaning / fakta pada conditional sentence tipe ke 2 dijabarkan memakai struktur simple present tense karena terkait kondisi berbanding terbalik dengan kenyataannya sekarang.

Sedangkan, jika pembahasan merujuk pada past unreal condition/ Untruth in the past, berarti ini merupakan conditional type ke 3. Pada umumnya meaning/fakta memakai struktur simple past tense karena terkait kondisi berbanding terbalik dengan kenyataanya di waktu lampau.

Explanation

CONDITIONAL SENTENCE/ ANDAI-ANDAI

Conditional sentence tells about the condition that will be actualized ( possible condition ) or only dreaming ( intention ) because of the particular condition.

Conditional sentence structure consists -If clause and result clause ( main clause ).

Use Comma when the result clause (main clause ) is started at the beginning sentence. Otherwise, there is no comma when -IF clause is put after the result clause.

There are 4 types of Conditional Sentence

1. CONDITIONAL ZEROConditional zero is used to describe the PHENOMENON,  and GENERAL TRUTH ( THE FACT/REALITY  )Conditional zero uses the simple present tense form to reflect the truth.

Pattern :

IF+S+base verb (s/es ) + O/C , S+base verb (s/es ) + O/C

Example :

If we boil water , it evaporates up into atmosphere.  ( Meaning : it is related to the fact. It is proven that the water evaporates up and disappear  if we boil water at 100 degrees Celsius )

2. TYPE 1 CONDITIONAL The type 1 Conditional is used to describe the truth  in the present/future ( The planning (prediction)  will be probably happened )In fact , the speaker will actualize his/her dream now or soon .

Pattern :

If +S+ verb (s/es) + O, S+will+verb1+O/C

Example :

If I study hard, I will pass the exam easily. ( Meaning : It's possible that I will be successful for the exam later

3. TYPE 2 CONDITIONAL The type 2 Conditional  is used to describe  the untruth in the present / futureIt is Contrary to the fact/ real condition in the present or it could be in the futureThe speaker has an intention ( dream ) but he/she can't afford his/her dream comes true in the present/future

Pattern:

If+S+verb2+O/C , S+Would/Could/might+verb1+O/C

Example :

If I were a president, I would build the biggest library in Jakarta. ( Meaning :  I can't build the biggest library because I'm not a president now )

4. TYPE 3 CONDITIONAL The type 3 Conditional  Is used to describe  the untruth in the pastIt is Contrary to the fact in the past

Pattern :

If+S+Had+Verb 3+O/C  , S+Would/Could+have+Verb 3+O/C

Example :

If I had known my friend's house address correctly, I wouldn't have been lost.  ( Meaning  : I didn't know my friend's house address so I was lost. )

RELATIVE PRONOUNS

1) WHO

Digunakan untuk menjabarkan orang (people) dan berfungsi sebagai pengganti posisi subyek.

Contoh:

The girl who spoke about the boy is standing over there.

2) WHICH

Digunakan untuk menjabarkan kata benda dan hewan. Berfungsi pengganti posisi subyek, obyek.

Contoh:

Give me some food which  I may eat.

Digunakan untuk memodifikasi kepemilikan obyek. Whose menggantikan possessive adjective determiner [her, his, their and etc]

Contoh:

That is the little girl whose mother was killed in traffic accident.

4. WHERE  

Digunakan untuk memodifikasi tempat/lokasi (city, country, house)

Contoh:

His house where he was born was on fire last night.

14. What are conditional sentences, and how are they structured in English? Can you explain the basic components of a conditional sentence, including the "if" and the main clauses?


Jawaban:

Conditional sentences are used to express a condition and its potential result. They typically consist of two main clauses: the "if" clause (conditional clause) and the main clause (result clause). The structure of a conditional sentence depends on the type of condition and its likelihood of occurring. There are generally four types: zero, first, second, and third conditionals.

1. **Zero Conditional:**

- **Structure:** If + present simple, present simple

- **Example:** If it rains, the streets flood.

2. **First Conditional:**

- **Structure:** If + present simple, will + base verb

- **Example:** If it rains, I will stay at home.

3. **Second Conditional:**

- **Structure:** If + past simple, would + base verb

- **Example:** If I won the lottery, I would buy a new car.

4. **Third Conditional:**

- **Structure:** If + past perfect, would have + past participle

- **Example:** If she had studied, she would have passed the exam.

In conditional sentences:

- The "if" clause introduces the condition and uses a specific verb tense.

- The main clause expresses the result or consequence and uses another verb tense.

- The use of "will," "would," and "would have" indicates the degree of likelihood or certainty regarding the result.

It's important to note that the zero conditional suggests real and timeless situations, the first conditional deals with real and possible future situations, the second conditional deals with unreal or unlikely present or future situations, and the third conditional deals with unreal, hypothetical past situations.

Understanding conditional sentences allows for precise communication about hypothetical or likely outcomes based on certain conditions being met.

30. 50 soal serta jawaban tentang conditional sentence ?


Conditional sentence is a sentence that expresses a condition. A condition is something that can only happen IF something else occurs. Conditional sentences are made up of a dependent clause and an independent clause joined to express said condition.

Pembahasan

Conditional Sentence Type 1: It is possible and also very likely that the condition will be fulfilled.

Conditional Sentence Type 2: It is possible but very unlikely, that the condition will be fulfilled.

Conditional Sentence Type 3: It is impossible that the condition will be fulfilled because it refers to the past.

Contoh soal tentang conditional sentences yaitu

Conditional sentence type 1:

1. If we ... (not/work) harder, we ... (not pass) the exam.

2. If she ... (not go) to the meeting, I ....(not go) either.

3. If the teacher ... (give) us lots of homework this weekend, I ... (not be) happy.

4. If the children ... (not eat) soon, they ... (be) grumpy.

5. If I ... (not go) to bed soon, I ... (be) tired in the morning.

6. If John .... (drink) too much coffee, he ... (get) ill.

7. If she ...(go) to the library, she ... (study) more.

8. If they ... (not/arrive) soon, we ... (be) late.

9. If you ... (buy) the present, I ... (wrap) it up.

34. soal dan jaaaawaban conditional centences


I.) 0 conditional
1.) If you ... (press) this key, the game ...(start). 
Jawaban: If you press this key, the game starts.

2.) If you ... (use) hot iron on nylon, it ...(melt).
Jawaban: If you use hot iron on nylon, it melts.

3.) The video ... (pause) if you ... (touch) this button.
Jawaban: The video pauses if you touch this button.

4.) If water ... (be) frozen, it ... (expand).
Jawaban: If water is frozen, it expands.
II.) 1st conditional
1.) If you ... (come) home late,  ... (not, wake) us up!
Jawaban: If you come home late, don't wake us up!

2.) I ... (Call) the police if you ... (not, leave) now!
Jawaban: I will call (I'll call) the police if you don't leave now!

3.) You ... (enjoy) Hamilton: An American Musical and Les Miserable if you ... (Like) good musicals.
Jawaban: You will enjoy Hamilton: An American Musical and Les Miserable if you like good musicals.

4.) Jovan ... (come) if you ... (promise) not to talk about mathematics all the time.
Jawaban: Jovan will come if you promise not to talk about mathematics all the time.

5.) If she ... (read) the book carefully, she ... (understand) it.
Jawaban: If she reads the book carefully, she will understand it.

6.) If I ... (pass) this calculus test, I ... (not, have) to study at the weekend.
Jawaban:  If I pass this calculus test, I will not have (I won't have) to study at the weekend.

III.) 2nd Conditional
1.) She ... (take) up jogging if she ... (not, have) breathing problem.
Jawaban: She would take up jogging if she didn't have breathing problem.

2.) If I ... (be) you, I ... (not, buy) that ticket to the concert.
Jawaban: If I were you, I Would not (wouldn't) buy that ticket to the concert

3.) I ... (help) you if I ... (be) free on Sunday evening.
Jawaban: I Would (I'd) help you if I were free on Sunday evening.

4.) If I ... (be) a rich man, I ...(build) a big tall house with rooms by the dozen. (Inspirasi dari Fiddler on the roof).
Jawaban: If I were a rich man, I would (I'd) build a big tall house with rooms by the dozen.

5.) If I ... (know) the composer of Master of The House in Les Miserable, I ... (tell) you.
Jawaban: If I knew the composer of  Master of The House in Les Miserable, I would tell you.

6.) Where ... (you, choose) if you ... (can) live anywhere in the world?
Jawaban: Where would you choose if you could live anywhere in the world?

7.) ... (it) be all right if I ... (use) your calculator?
Jawaban: Would it be all right if I used your calculator?
